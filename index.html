Research Focus

Our multidisciplinary research spans vision, language, graphs, and large‑scale data mining.

Computer Vision & Identity Systems

Face recognition, biometric verification, multi‑modal identity detection, deepfake detection, and surveillance analytics using state‑of‑the‑art CNNs and transformers.

Machine Learning in Drug Discovery

Molecular property prediction, protein‑ligand binding estimation, generative models for novel compound design, and bioinformatics pipelines.

Fraud Detection & Risk Intelligence

Anomaly detection, transaction graph mining, explainable fraud scoring, and real‑time monitoring systems.

Large‑Scale Opinion Mining

Social network data indexing, political inclination inference, sentiment analysis, and multi‑modal opinion modeling using posts, text, and images.
